Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Peelr (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our AI shopping assistant. Peelr is operated by Peel AI LLC in New Jersey, United States.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Email address (for login through Firebase Authentication)
  • Name or profile data from Google/Microsoft sign-in
  • Preferences and chat history (for better recommendations)

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address (via Cloudflare logs)
  • Device type, browser type, general location
  • Usage analytics and search activity
  • Cookies for ads, analytics, and personalization

C. AI Processing

Your search queries and product interactions may be processed by AI models (e.g., OpenAI) to generate recommendations. We do not sell your data to AI providers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized product recommendations
  • To improve search accuracy and UX
  • To monitor performance and prevent abuse
  • To authenticate user accounts
  • To support sponsored and affiliate programs

4. Advertising & Affiliate Partners

Peelr participates in affiliate and sponsored ad programs. We may earn commissions from clicks or purchases through external links.

Cookies & Ad Personalization

Third-party advertising networks (including Google Ad Manager) may use cookies to:

  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent repetitive ads
  • Personalize ads based on your browsing behavior

You can manage personalization settings at Google Ad Settings.

5. Data Sharing

We share information with:

  • Firebase (authentication)
  • Cloudflare (infrastructure and security)
  • OpenAI (AI processing)
  • Affiliate partners (click tracking only)

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Retention

We retain account data until you request deletion. Anonymous analytics logs may be stored for security and performance purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may request:

  • Access to your stored data
  • Correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising

8. Children’s Privacy

Peelr is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.
